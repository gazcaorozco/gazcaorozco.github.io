About Me

Hello! I am Alexei Gazca (aka Pablo Alexei Gazca Orozco), currently a researcher at the Mathematical Institute at Charles University in Prague. My reseach interests lie in general in the numerical analysis of nonlinear PDE, including in particular:

  • Non-Newtonian fluids and nonlinear elasticity
  • Minimal regularity error estimates
  • Nonlinear boundary conditions
  • A posteriori analysis via equilibrated fluxes and duality techniques
